# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Modelable should be a productive, respectful place to collaborate. Be clear,
patient, and constructive. Critique ideas and code, not people. Assume good
faith while remaining direct about technical risk.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
unwelcome sexual attention, deliberate intimidation, publishing private
information, or repeatedly disrupting project work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and other
contributions that violate this standard. They may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Do
not use the public issue tracker for sensitive reports. Maintainers will
review reports promptly, preserve confidentiality where possible, and apply
proportionate action.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is representing the
project in public.
